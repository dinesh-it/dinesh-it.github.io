Privacy Policy

CashCounter Mobile Application

Last updated: September 15, 2025

About CashCounter

CashCounter is a mobile application that helps users count and manage cash denominations. The app provides two calculation modes: Count to Total (input quantities to calculate total amount) and Total to Count (input target amount to get optimal denomination breakdown).

Developer: Dinesh Dharmalingam

App ID: com.dineshdharmalingam.cashcounter

Privacy Summary: CashCounter is designed with privacy in mind. All your calculations and data remain on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or calculation data to external servers.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account creation or sign-up required
  • No personal identification information collected
  • No calculation data or amounts are transmitted to our servers
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No advertising or marketing data collection

Data Storage and Processing

Local Storage Only

All app functionality operates entirely on your device:

  • Settings: Your language preferences and denomination settings are stored locally using device storage
  • Calculations: All cash counting calculations are performed locally on your device
  • No Cloud Sync: No data is synchronized to cloud services

Data That Stays on Your Device

  • Language preference (English/Tamil)
  • Default denomination preferences
  • Currency selection settings
  • Current calculation session data (cleared when app is closed)

App Permissions

CashCounter may request the following permissions for functionality:

  • Share/Export: To share calculation results via your device's native sharing functionality
  • Storage: To save your app preferences locally on your device

These permissions are used solely for app functionality and do not involve data transmission to external servers.

Third-Party Services

CashCounter does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. The app operates independently without external dependencies that could access your data.

Data Security

Since all data remains on your device:

  • Your calculation data is as secure as your device
  • No risk of data breaches from our servers (we don't have any)
  • You maintain full control over your data
  • Uninstalling the app removes all associated data

Children's Privacy

CashCounter does not collect any information from users of any age. The app is safe for users of all ages as it operates entirely offline with no data collection.

International Users

Since no data is collected or transmitted, CashCounter complies with international privacy regulations including GDPR, CCPA, and other regional privacy laws by design.

Changes to Privacy Policy

Any updates to this privacy policy will be reflected in the app and on this page. Since we don't collect contact information, we recommend checking this page periodically for updates. Material changes will be highlighted in app update notes.

Open Source

CashCounter's commitment to privacy can be verified as the source code may be made available for inspection, confirming that no data collection or transmission occurs.
