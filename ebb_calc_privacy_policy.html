Privacy Policy

EB Bill Calculator App

Last Updated: December 2024
Quick Summary: EB Bill Calculator is a privacy-focused app that stores all your electricity usage data locally on your device. We don't collect, store, or share your personal information or usage data with any third parties.

1. Information We Collect

EB Bill Calculator is designed with privacy in mind:

  • Local Data Only: All your meter readings, bill information, and usage calculations are stored locally on your device
  • No Personal Information: We don't collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personally identifiable information
  • No Usage Tracking: We don't track how you use the app or what features you access
  • No Analytics: We don't use analytics services that collect user behavior data

2. Data Storage

Your data privacy is our top priority:

  • Device Storage: All data is stored locally using your device's secure storage system
  • No Cloud Sync: Your data never leaves your device or gets uploaded to any servers
  • No Backups: We don't create backups of your data on external servers
  • Data Control: You have complete control over your data and can delete it anytime

3. Data We Don't Collect

We explicitly do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone)
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Contacts or other apps on your device
  • Camera, microphone, or file access data
  • Usage patterns or app behavior analytics

4. Third-Party Services

EB Bill Calculator may include:

  • AdMob (Google): For displaying advertisements. AdMob may collect device information and ad interaction data according to Google's privacy policy
  • App Stores: Download and update information is handled by Google Play Store or Apple App Store according to their respective privacy policies

These third-party services operate independently and have their own privacy policies.

5. Advertising

Our app may display advertisements through Google AdMob:

  • Ad providers may use device identifiers to serve relevant ads
  • No personal data from our app is shared with advertisers
  • You can opt-out of personalized advertising through your device settings
  • Ad interactions are handled entirely by the ad provider

6. Permissions

The app may request minimal permissions:

  • Storage: To save your meter data locally on your device
  • Network: Only for displaying advertisements (if applicable)

All permissions are used solely for app functionality and not for data collection.

7. Data Security

We implement security measures:

  • All data is stored using your device's secure storage mechanisms
  • No data transmission to external servers reduces security risks
  • App follows mobile security best practices
  • Regular security updates through app store updates

8. Children's Privacy

EB Bill Calculator is safe for all ages:

  • We don't knowingly collect information from children under 13
  • No personal information is collected from any users
  • The app contains no inappropriate content
  • Local storage ensures no data leaves the device

9. Data Deletion

You have complete control over your data:

  • App Deletion: Uninstalling the app removes all your data
  • In-App Deletion: Use the history feature to delete specific readings
  • Reset Option: Clear all data through app settings (if available)
  • No Recovery: Once deleted, data cannot be recovered as we don't store backups

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy:

  • Changes will be reflected in app updates
  • Major changes will be highlighted in update notes
  • Continued use constitutes acceptance of changes
  • You can always review the current policy in the app

11. Tamil Nadu Electricity Board (TNEB)

Important clarification:

  • This app is not affiliated with Tamil Nadu Electricity Board (TNEB)
  • This is an independent utility app for personal bill calculations
  • Tariff rates are based on publicly available TNEB information
  • For official TNEB services, visit their official website or offices

12. Disclaimer

Please note:

  • Bill calculations are estimates based on current tariff structures
  • Actual bills may vary due to various factors and additional charges
  • Always refer to official TNEB bills for accurate amounts
  • Use this app as a planning and estimation tool only

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or the app, please contact us through:

  • App Store reviews and ratings
  • In-app feedback options (if available)

Note: Since we don't collect personal information, we cannot respond to individual inquiries via email or other personal channels.
